About

Hello! My name is Bec Nichele Smith. I'm a senior studying Science, Technology, and Society at Stanford.

My major combines computer science and humanities (history, anthropology, psychology, communications, etc.) to provide insight into users and their interactions with technology. I've been trained to investigate, understand, and present the ways personal and cultural values affect the ways users interact with technology, as well as how technology informs the actions of users.
